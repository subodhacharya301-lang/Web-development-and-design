Damauli, Tanahun

Damauli is the administrative headquarters of the Tanahun district in Gandaki Province, Nepal, located within the Vyas Municipality on the banks of the Madi and Seti rivers. Positioned along the Prithvi Highway, it is a bustling, ethnically diverse city (~70,335 residents) known for its scenic hills, cultural heritage, and as the birthplace of poet Bhanubhakta Acharya.
