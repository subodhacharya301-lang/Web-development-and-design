Bartika Eam Rai

Bartika Eam Rai is a prominent New York-based Nepali singer-songwriter who rose to fame in 2016 with her soulful debut single "Khai" and her first album, Bimbaakash. Originally from Lalitpur, Nepal, she began her musical training in Eastern vocals at age 10 before moving to the U.S. in 2015, where she bridged the Kathmandu underground scene with professional production through frequent collaborations with musician Diwas Gurung. Known for her introspective and poetic style, her discography includes acclaimed albums like Taral and Si Ma Na, which have earned her multiple accolades, including the Hits FM Music Awards for Best Pop Vocal Performance (Female) and Song of the Year. Today, she remains a key figure in contemporary Nepali pop, exploring themes of nostalgia and love while maintaining a close connection with her audience through her social media presence.
